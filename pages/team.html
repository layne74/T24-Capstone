MEET THE TEAM

I race under the team name HSC Racing which stands for Hutchings Service Centre Racing. Since my father owns HSC, we incorperated it into our racing. We have a car and bike department. My father, Sandro and myself race cars in Classics.

Sandro has been racing for roughly 12 years. He started with a Volkswagen Scirocco mk1 and built it up over time. That car lasted longer than it should as it saw quite a few crashes. Unfortunatly About a year ago the car was written off, one last crash and boy, was it spectacular. Sandro was very lucky to walk away from it. Fortunatly he has another racing car that he has been using since the accident. Trevor, my father, has been racing for 8 years. He too races a Scirocco mk1. That car has also seen numerous accidents, fortunatly, touch wood, none bad enough to write it off. I have been racing for 4 years, I race a Volkswagen Golf Mk2. I wrote my first car off due to steering failure, and built another one in about a week as I was in contention for overall podium.

And then in the bike department, we have my brother(Braddon Hutchings), Abigail Bosson and my Nicholas Hutchings which is my cousin.

Braddon started racing about 3 years ago on short circuit with the cbr 150. He later converted a Kawasaki Ninja 300 into a track bike. He is still building it up to spec at the moment and is hoping to be more competitive soon. Nicholas has been racing short circuit on a cbr 150 for about 4 years. He has just recently moved to main circuit on a ninja 300 as well. Abigail has been racing for 6 years. She started on little NSR 50 race bikes and then moved to the 150 as she got older.
